Food Cart

FoodCart is a full-stack food delivery web application inspired by Zomato, designed to provide a seamless online food ordering experience. It features an intuitive user interface where users can browse restaurants, explore menus, add items to their cart, and place orders effortlessly. Integrated with a secure payment gateway, FoodCart ensures smooth and safe transactions. The platform also includes real-time order tracking, user authentication, and an admin panel for restaurant management. Built for efficiency and scalability, FoodCart enhances the food delivery experience for both customers and vendors.
